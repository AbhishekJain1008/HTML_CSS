OutDoor Sports:

Outdoor games are sports or physical activities that are played in open spaces such as playgrounds, fields, or stadiums. These games require physical strength, stamina, teamwork, and strategy. Outdoor games help players stay active, healthy, and energetic.

Playing outdoor games allows people to connect with nature, enjoy fresh air, and improve both physical and mental fitness. These games are popular among children, teenagers, and professional athletes.
